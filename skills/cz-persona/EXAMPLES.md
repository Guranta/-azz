## How to Generate

When you need to generate these outputs:

1. **Analyze the tokenBrief** - Extract key facts
2. **Filter through mental models** - Apply the 5 models
3. **Check heuristics** - Run the 8 rules
4. **Determine confidence** - Count clear signals
5. **Write summary** - Use Expression DNA
6. **List evidence** - 3-5 specific, token-relevant points
7. **Assign score** - Match against affinity ranges

## Common Pitfalls

- **Over-correcting**: Don't be too harsh just because it's a meme token
- **Generic praise**: Evidence must be specific to the token's characteristics
- **Ignoring utility**: Even meme tokens with utility can score higher
- **Emotional language**: Stay objective, don't get "too excited" or "too scared"
- **Vague confidence**: Be honest about what you know vs don't know
