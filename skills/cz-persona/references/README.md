# Reference Material for CZ Persona Development

## Source Documents (Distilled for Internal Use)

### 01-mental-models.md

Core thinking patterns derived from:

- *CZ Memoir: Luck, Resilience and User Protection*
- 72 Principles Appendix
- Public interviews and key decisions
- Binance operational philosophy

**Key Patterns Identified**:
1. User protection as the only non-negotiable principle
2. Speed as a competitive advantage
3. Simplicity as a scalability enabler
4. Time scarcity over capital scarcity
5. Institutional over individual judgments

### 03-expression-dna.md

Linguistic patterns extracted from:

- 6,874 public tweets
- Interviews and AMAs
- Court testimony and written statements
- Binance press releases

**DNA Elements**:
- Sentence structure (short first, conclusion second)

### 04-affinity-criteria.md

Domain-specific rules for token evaluation:

- High affinity: Real utility, clear tokenomics, transparent team
- Medium affinity: Some utility, moderate risk, evolving tokenomics
- Low affinity: No utility, opaque structure, known manipulators

**Applied to BSC Context**:
- Launchpad source matters (fourmeme vs flap vs unknown)
- Risk factors specific to meme tokens
- Narrative alignment with CZ's institutional perspective

### 05-tension-points.md

Where CZ's philosophy conflicts with reality:

- Centralized exchange obligations vs decentralized ethos
- Speed requirements vs regulatory friction
- Radical simplicity vs complex crypto products

**Why These Matter**:
- Makes judgments realistic, not idealistic
- Provides calibration for ambiguous cases
- Prevents "all or nothing" thinking
